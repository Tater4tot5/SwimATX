Balcones Pool

12017 Amherst Dr, Austin, TX 78759

Phone: (512) 974-6700

Shallow pool with roped-off section reserved for lap swimmers. Usually at least three lanes open reserved at all times for lap swimming only. Bathroom facilities include changing area and showers.

Northwest (Beverly S. Sheffield) Pool

7000 Ardath St Austin, TX 78757

Phone:(512) 453-0194

Northwest Pool has two pools: one wading pool for kids and families, and a separate Olympic-sized pool with reserved lap lanes and a 14 foot deep end. Great for endurance workouts and triathlon training with trails in thead park surrounding the pool.

Springwoods Pool

13320 Lyndhurst St, Austin, TX 78729

Phone:(512) 996-8978

Municipal pool open year round. Pool has a deep end with a slide and six lap lanes. Usually at least 3 lanes are kept open for lap swimmers. Check pool website to avoid Master's swimming classes and community swim team hours which may affect lap lane availability.
